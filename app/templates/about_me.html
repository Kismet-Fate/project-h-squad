About Me

I’m the type of person that will burst out laughing in dead silence over something that happened yesterday. My ethics are so clear and transparent that you can’t even see them. Sometimes I wish my mouth had a Pause button… I’m so cool I wasn’t actually born, I was defrosted. I’m so cool that even ice cubes are jealous. I’m so hot that I contribute to global warming. I am so clever that sometimes I don’t understand a single word of what I am saying. I don’t believe in miracles. I rely on them. I’m painting a blue square in my backyard so Google Earth thinks I have a pool.
